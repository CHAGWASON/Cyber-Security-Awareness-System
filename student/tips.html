Stay Safe Online

Follow these essential cybersecurity practices to protect your data and identity.

Cyber threats are increasing daily. Always stay alert and practice safe online behavior.
Password Security
  • Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
  • Never reuse passwords across multiple platforms
  • Change passwords regularly
  • Use a password manager
  • Avoid saving passwords on public computers
Phishing Awareness
  • Do not click suspicious links
  • Verify email senders before responding
  • Avoid downloading unknown attachments
  • Watch out for urgent or threatening messages
  • Check website URLs before entering credentials
Device Security
  • Install antivirus and anti-malware software
  • Keep OS and apps updated
  • Use screen lock (PIN, password, fingerprint)
  • Avoid installing untrusted apps
  • Enable firewall protection
Internet Safety
  • Avoid sensitive transactions on public Wi-Fi
  • Use VPN when necessary
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Only visit secure websites (https://)
  • Clear browser history on public systems
Data Protection
  • Backup important files regularly
  • Use cloud storage securely
  • Encrypt sensitive data
  • Avoid sharing confidential information
  • Secure your USB/external drives
Social Media Safety
  • Limit personal information shared online
  • Adjust privacy settings on accounts
  • Avoid accepting unknown friend requests
  • Be cautious of fake profiles
  • Do not share location publicly

💡 Staying informed is your first defense against cyber threats.
